Strelitzia Nicolai, is a species of evergreen tropical herbaceous plant, native to South Africa. It has broad and large green leaves and can sometimes, rarely indoors, produce beautiful, white bird-like flowers, like birds of paradise.

Strelitzia Reginae, is a species of evergreen tropical herbaceous plant, native to South Africa. It has narrow, clumping green leaves that are quite a bit smaller than its display of orange and blue bird-like flowers.

Hoya 'Mathilde' is a cross between Hoya carnosa and Hoya serpens. It's ovate leaves are small and succulent and covered in silver spots. White, whimsically fuzzy flowers appear in spherical clusters and exude a syrupy sweet fragrance that will delight the senses.
